Filipino Wedding Traditions

These Filipino wedding traditions symbolize unity, prosperity, and the couple’s lifelong commitment to one another.

Veil Cord Candles Arras Money Dance

The Veil

The veil is draped over the couple to symbolize their unity as one. It represents harmony, protection, and a shared future together.

The Cord

The cord, shaped like a figure eight, represents an unbreakable bond and the everlasting nature of marriage.

The Candles

The lighting of the candles symbolizes the light that will guide the couple through their journey as husband and wife.

The Arras (Coins)

The groom presents coins to the bride, symbolizing trust, shared responsibility, and prosperity in marriage.

The Money Dance

Guests pin money onto the couple while dancing to wish them good fortune, abundance, and happiness in their married life.
